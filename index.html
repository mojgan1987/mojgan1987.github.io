Background

My path started with a strong foundation in computer engineering and artificial intelligence, followed by a PhD that combined AI with human behaviour and interaction. Since then, I have moved between research and industry, always with the same aim: to build systems that are technically sound, understandable and useful.

I enjoy bringing clarity to complex problems, whether that is through a well-designed model, a clear visualisation or a precise explanation for non-technical stakeholders.

Beyond work

Outside of work, I enjoy nature and look after a small garden. Hiking and travelling help me reset and keep perspective. I also stay connected to the research community by volunteering as a reviewer, which allows me to support others and keep in touch with new ideas.
